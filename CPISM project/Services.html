Welcome To Zara Therapy Clinic

Physiotherapy Services at Zara Therapy Clinic provide a compassionate and comprehensive program for adults and children in overcoming physical, neurological, cognitive impairments and disabilities caused by injury or disease. Our physiotherapy practices meet International Quality Standards such as Joint Commission International (JCI).

All our physiotherapists are highly trained, equipped with sophisticated equipment and work within an interdisciplinary medical team to provide examination, evaluation, diagnosis, prognosis and interventions towards achieving the treatment goals of every patient. The program is designed to restore the patient’s physical functions, relieve pain and minimise the psychological, social and vocational impact of the injury or disease. Patients can access our services by visiting the clinic (outpatients) or are offered the service if they are admitted to the hospital (inpatients) or through our outreach medical centres and home health services where we will come to your doorstep. ​


Our Physiotherapy Services

Our Physiotherapy Services at The Aga Khan University Hospital offers a range of services.


Speech Therapy

Our Speech Therapists are qualified professionals with specialized training to provide therapy to patients who have issues with hearing, visual comprehension (understand images and words), reading comprehension (understand what you are reading), oral motor strengthening (improve range of motion and coordination of the lips, tongue and jaw), swallowing, verbal expression and voice characteristics.

Our Speech Therapy Clinic offers professional and specialized treatment for children and adults with the following speech disorders but not limited to; articulation disorder, stuttering, voice, difficulty with speech or swallowing, language skills, aphasia.


Occupational Therapy

Our Occupational Therapists are professionally trained to assess and treat patients with a physical, mental, or cognitive disorder to help develop, recover, or maintain their daily living and work skills. Our therapists work with patients not only to improve their basic motor functions and reasoning abilities, but also to compensate for permanent loss of function.​​
